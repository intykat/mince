# About
Conquer the complexities of game networking with this lightweight framework. Mince handles the heavy lifting, allowing you to seamlessly connect players and build immersive multiplayer experiences with ease.  Beyond networking, Mince offers powerful script control and an optimized workflow to maximize your development potential.

### Organization
At the heart of Mince lies a powerful declarative system. Simply define where and when your code should execute, and Mince handles the underlying logic. This approach fosters clean, well-structured codebases, leading to enhanced organization and effortless maintainability.  Focus on crafting your game logic, not managing its execution.

### Seamless Networking 
Mince eliminates the complexities of traditional Roblox networking. Forget manually creating RemoteEvents and RemoteFunctions. Mince provides an intuitive, built-in networking layer that streamlines communication between client and server, allowing you to focus on building engaging multiplayer experiences.
